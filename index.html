About Us

At our company, we're passionate about helping you enjoy a clean and fresh space without the stress. Founded by Karen, a dedicated mother of five, we understand just how demanding it can be to keep a home or workspace spotless. Karen's experience as a busy mom inspired her to create a cleaning service that makes life easier for families, professionals, and anyone looking to reclaim their time.

With over three years in business, we are proud of the glowing reviews from our satisfied customers. We know that a clean environment can transform your day, and that’s why we pour our heart into every job. Our team is known for attention to detail—because we believe that the little things matter. At the same time, we prioritize efficiency, making sure you get the best value from our services without sacrificing quality.

Nothing makes us happier than exceeding our clients' expectations. We love the satisfaction of seeing our customers walk into a space that feels fresh, inviting, and completely spotless. Whether it's your home, office, or any other space, we’re here to do the heavy lifting so you can enjoy the results.

We invite you to experience the difference for yourself. Give us a try, and we’re confident you won’t be disappointed. Feel free to check out the reviews from our many happy customers—we can't wait to make you one of them!

OUR PRICING IS SIMPLE.

We keep pricing simple: you pay by the cleaning time for a team of two cleaners. Contact us for our hourly rate and a time estimate tailored to your cleaning needs. You’ll be pleasantly surprised by how affordable we are! If we are done cleaning in 1 hour and 20 minutes. We will charge you exactly that.

Our Services

These are our most common services but we are flexible and willing to work with you with any of your cleaning needs. Just let us know.

Basic Cleaning

Our most common service for recurring cleaning services. We focus on maintaining your house/workspace clean and fresh by dusting, vacuuming/mopping, and cleaning surfaces in general.

Deep Cleaning

In addition to our basic cleaning, we will scrub, wipe baseboards, detail doors, walls, light fixtures, etc. This is a detailed cleaning and we are happy to include ovens, refrigerators, and any other appliances that you want us to clean.

Move In / Move Out.

Similar to a deep cleaning service, we will ensure that your home/office is ready for anyone to move in and enjoy your space without having to worry about cleaning and ready to hit the ground running.

Eco Friendly / Natural Products.

If you are looking for natural and eco friendly products, we have amazing products that will leave your place looking amazing while having peace of mind of the quality of our products. This service is extra and will be an additional fee to our regular pricing.
